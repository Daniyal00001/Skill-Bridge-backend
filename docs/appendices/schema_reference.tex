\chapter{Database Schema Quick Reference}
\label{app:schema}

This appendix provides a concise tabular reference for every model in the \projectname{} Prisma schema (\texttt{prisma/schema.prisma}).

\begin{table}[H]
\centering
\caption{All Database Models Summary}
\begin{tabularx}{\textwidth}{llX}
\toprule
\textbf{Model} & \textbf{Collection} & \textbf{Primary Purpose} \\
\midrule
User              & users               & Unified auth entity for all roles. \\
Session           & sessions            & Refresh token server-side storage. \\
ClientProfile     & client\_profiles    & Client-specific profile data. \\
FreelancerProfile & freelancer\_profiles & Freelancer-specific profile data. \\
AdminProfile      & admin\_profiles     & Admin account metadata. \\
Project           & projects            & Client project listings. \\
ProjectSkill      & project\_skills     & Many-to-many: Project $\leftrightarrow$ Skill. \\
Proposal          & proposals           & Freelancer bids on projects. \\
Contract          & contracts           & Active agreements from accepted proposals. \\
Milestone         & milestones          & Deliverable checkpoints within contracts. \\
Payment           & payments            & Payment/escrow status per milestone. \\
Dispute           & disputes            & Contract dispute records. \\
Review            & reviews             & Blind post-contract reviews. \\
ChatRoom          & chat\_rooms         & Messaging rooms per contract or pre-contract. \\
Message           & messages            & Individual chat messages. \\
Notification      & notifications       & In-app notification feed. \\
Skill             & skills              & Global skills catalogue. \\
FreelancerSkill   & freelancer\_skills  & Many-to-many: Freelancer $\leftrightarrow$ Skill. \\
PortfolioItem     & portfolio\_items    & Freelancer past work showcase. \\
Certificate       & certificates        & Freelancer professional credentials. \\
Education         & education           & Freelancer education history. \\
Gig               & gigs                & Freelancer pre-packaged services. \\
Category          & categories          & Project category taxonomy. \\
SubCategory       & sub\_categories     & Project sub-category taxonomy. \\
Invitation        & invitations         & Client-to-freelancer project invitations. \\
TokenTransaction  & token\_transactions & Immutable SkillToken ledger. \\
AiScopingSession  & ai\_scoping\_sessions & Stored AI assistant conversations. \\
SavedProject      & saved\_projects     & Freelancer project bookmarks. \\
BrowseInteraction & browse\_interactions & Browsing analytics for personalisation. \\
AdminLog          & admin\_logs         & Immutable admin action audit trail. \\
PlatformSetting   & platform\_settings  & Global key-value configuration. \\
PasswordResetToken & (embedded)         & Secure password reset tokens. \\
\bottomrule
\end{tabularx}
\label{tab:schema_summary}
\end{table}
